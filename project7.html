Project Overview

This is a lighting design project for a commercial building consisting of a ground floor, five typical floors, and a roof. The lighting simulation and layout were fully completed using DIALux Evo, in compliance with the Egyptian Code.

Lighting Design Stage

The design covers all floors and includes both normal and emergency lighting fixtures. Fixtures were carefully selected based on functionality and interior aesthetics, and include:

  • 60x60 LED panels
  • Circular spotlights
  • LED profiles
  • Pendant fixtures
  • Surface-mounted lights
  • Decorative chandeliers

Simulation results were optimized for lux levels and energy efficiency, forming a solid foundation for future load calculation and layout detailing.

Status
