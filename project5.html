Commercial Building

üè¢ Project Overview

This project involved the complete electrical design of a commercial building consisting of two floors and a roof, with an open space between the first and second floors (double-height area). The system design adhered strictly to the Egyptian code, ensuring proper lighting distribution, safety, and load management.

üí° Lighting Design

Lighting fixtures were selected based on room usage and classification of loads into Normal, Emergency, and UPS loads. Dialux Evo software was used to perform lighting calculations and ensure proper illumination levels in all areas.

Types of Fixtures Used:

  • 60x60 Surface Mounted
  • Circular LED (IP20/IP44)
  • Ceiling Mounted Linear LED Profile (IP44)
  • Wall Mounted Circular
  • Magnetic Track
  • Pendant LED Linear (IP44)
  • Circular Suspended LED
  • Wall Emergency LED for Stairs (IP44)
  • Exit Sign

üîå Electrical Distribution

The building includes three main distribution boards (MDB, SMDB, EMDB) and one ATS panel.

MDB supplies:

  • SMDB, EMDB, MCCs for RF/VRV & AHU
  • Two chillers
  • Elevator system

SMDB supplies:

  • Normal and HVAC DBs for each floor and the roof (DB-GF, DBHV-GF, DB-FF, DBHV-FF, DB-R)

Each DB includes proper breaker sizing and overload protection per Egyptian Code, with MCCBs in main boards and MCBs in sub-panels. Earth leakage protection was also applied where needed.

üîã Generator & Light Current

The generator sizing was done using Cummins software, ensuring capacity matches load demand.

The project also includes full Light Current systems with shop drawings for:

  • Fire Fighting
  • CCTV
  • Data & Telephone

üìê Drawings & Deliverables

  • Full Single Line Diagram (SLD) for the building and MCC panels
  • Load schedules with circuit breaker sizing
  • Complete lighting calculations (Dialux)
  • Shop drawings for light current systems
  • Generator sizing report

This project was fully executed individually, from concept to final design.
